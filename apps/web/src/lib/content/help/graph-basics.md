## Navigating Your World

The Knowledge Graph is the heart of Codex Cryptica. It shows you the relationships between every person, place, and event in your campaign.

### Mouse & Touch Controls

- **Click Node**: Select an entry to read it in the detail panel.
- **Scroll / Pinch**: Zoom in and out.
- **Right-Click Node**: Open the menu to Rename, Delete, or Merge nodes.
- **Drag Node**: Move nodes around to organize your view.
- **Drag Background**: Pan across the map.

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- `C`: Enter **Connect Mode** to link two nodes.
- `Space`: Re-center the view on your selected node.
- `F`: Toggle **Focus Mode** (hides everything except the selected node and its neighbors).
- `L`: Show or hide connection labels.
- `Esc`: Clear your selection or exit modes.
