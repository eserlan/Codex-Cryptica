# Quickstart: Proactive Lore Discovery

## Enabling Auto-Archive

1. Open **Settings** from the sidebar or via `Cmd/Ctrl + ,`.
2. Navigate to the **Oracle** tab.
3. Toggle **Auto-Archive Conversational Entities** to "On".
4. (Optional) Set the **Draft Status Visibility** to "Hidden" if you want a completely silent background archive.

## Using Discovery Chips

When the Oracle describes something new:

1. Look for a chip at the bottom of the chat message: `[+] Found: Thalindra (NPC)`.
2. **Click to Commit**: Click the `[+]` to instantly add it to your vault.
3. **Review**: Click the entity name to open a preview window, adjust the Lore or Chronicle, and then click **Verify**.
4. **Smart Update**: If you're talking about an existing character, look for `[~] Update: Thalindra`. Clicking it will append the new facts to her record.

## Managing Drafts

1. Open the **Entity Explorer**.
2. Use the **Status** filter to show only "Draft" entries.
3. Batch select drafts to **Verify** or **Delete**.
