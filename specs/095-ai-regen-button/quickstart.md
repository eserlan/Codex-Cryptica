# Quickstart: AI Entity Regeneration

## Overview

The AI Regeneration feature allows Hosts/GMs to quickly generate or refine entity descriptions. It produces two versions: one for players (Chronicle) and one for the GM (Lore).

## Usage

1.  **Open an Entity**: Navigate to any entity in the Sidepanel or Zen Mode.
2.  **Trigger AI**: Click the "AI Regenerate" button (icon: `lucide--sparkles` or similar).
3.  **Review Preview**:
    - The entity fields will be highlighted.
    - Proposed text will appear in the "Content" and "Lore" tabs.
    - Note: The fields are read-only during this preview.
4.  **Finalize**:
    - Click **Save** to commit the AI content to your vault.
    - Click **Discard** to revert to your original text.

## Requirements

- Active AI Subscription (Tier: Advanced).
- Host/GM Permissions.
- Internet Connection.
